How to Play Burst

Burst is a competitive tactical trick-taking bidding card game played over 4 rounds. Your goal is to predict and win the exact number of tricks you bid. If you do, you secure a massive scoring bonus. If you win more or fewer, you "burst" and receive 0 points!

1. Rounds and Dealing

The tournament runs for 4 rounds. The hand sizes scale up each round:

  • Round 1: 3 cards
  • Round 2: 5 cards
  • Round 3: 8 cards
  • Round 4: 10 cards

2. Bidding Phase

At the start of each round, a random Trump Suit is selected. Review your hand and bid between 0 and the number of cards in your hand. This is the exact number of tricks you aim to win.

3. Gameplay Tricks

A player leads the first trick. You must follow the led suit if you have cards of that suit. If not, you may play any card, including a Trump card. The trick is won by the highest card of the led suit, or by the highest Trump card if a trump is played.

Card ranking: A (highest) > K > Q > J > 10 > ... > 2 (lowest).

4. Scoring System

At the end of the round, tricks won are checked against your bid:

  • Hit Bid Exactly: You gain 10 + (11 × Bid) points.
  • Miss Bid (Burst): You receive 0 points.
